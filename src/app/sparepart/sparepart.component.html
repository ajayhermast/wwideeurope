Wwidurope provides reverse engineering spare parts, for machinery in Oil & Gas and power sector services for rotatory plant equipment like turbines and compressors.


We have a team of qualified engineers and technical consultants with full capability to help and support customers by providing parts, technical assistance, consultancy, refurbishment, or overhauling of parts or complete units.


With know-how and knowledge of Gas and Steam Turbines machines, supply chains, and manufacturing processes, we are active in Europe and the Middle East.




Gas Turbine Spare Parts: Offering high-quality, precision-engineered spare parts for gas turbines, ensuring reliable and efficient operation of your equipment.


Steam Turbine Spare Parts: Supplying durable and precise steam turbine components that meet industry standards for optimal performance and longevity.


Generator Spare Parts: Providing spare parts for generators, ensuring minimal downtime and continued operation.


Special Tools and Equipment: Supplying specialized tools and equipment necessary for the maintenance and repair of turbines, compressors, and other critical machinery.


Spare Parts Consulting and Management: Offering expert consultation on spare parts management, including the identification, sourcing, and inventory management of critical components.


Repairing and Refurbishment: Providing comprehensive repair and refurbishment services for turbines, compressors, and related equipment to restore them to optimal working condition.


Reverse Engineering: Leveraging advanced reverse engineering techniques to produce high-quality spare parts for machinery, even when original parts are unavailable or obsolete.


Root Cause Analysis: Conducting thorough investigations into equipment failure to identify root causes, followed by the implementation of effective corrective measures.


Technical Consulting and Specifications Issuing: Offering expert technical consulting services and issuing detailed specifications for parts, repairs, and overhauling processes, ensuring the highest standards are met.
